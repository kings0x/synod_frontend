**Fonts:**
Headline font: Oswald (700 weight)
A free Google Font. Heavy, condensed, geometric, all-caps. This is what gives the headline its aggressive industrial weight — similar to the Bebas Neue / Tungsten family. Loaded via @import from Google Fonts.
Subtext / UI font: Space Mono (400 + 700 weight)
Also a free Google Font. Monospace — every character is the same width, giving that terminal/developer/infrastructure feel. Used for the navbar links, badge text, subheadline paragraph, button labels, and scroll hint. This is the font that communicates "this is a technical product."
